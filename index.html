About WIBFAIR

WIBFair: Empowering Women in Business

WIBFair is a brainchild of Ebiere Empere, the visionary CEO of Okads Seafood and founder of Okads Women in Business Initiative. This innovative platform was born out of Okads' passion to empower women entrepreneurs and provide a supportive ecosystem for their growth.

WIBFair is dedicated to:
- Fostering connections and collaborations among women in business
- Providing access to resources, mentorship, and funding opportunities
- Celebrating women's achievements and successes
- Creating a supportive community that inspires and motivates

Join us at WIBFair, a transformative movement where women lift each other up and strive for excellence together!

Event Details:
- Date: 1st - 3rd November 2024
- Venue: Old Parade Ground
- Time: 9am - 10pm
